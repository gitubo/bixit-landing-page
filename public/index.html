How It Works

Focus on your business logic, not the mediation layer

counter_1

Integrate the library

Integrate the open-source Bixit library into your app using just one function. Your business logic can start accessing human-readable JSON structure instead of manipulating a long stream of bits.

counter_2

Define Your Formats

Prepare simple config files describing the message structures you want to handle. Take advantage of a no-code approch to help domain expert in designing and testing complex low level format.

counter_3

Upload configuration

Deploy your config files just copying them into a directory you application can access. Let Bixit automatically load and use your catalog without the need to recompile anything: maintanance is a breeze.
